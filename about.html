About EA991 Driving Academy

Leading driving education in Gaborone since 2015

Our Mission

EA991 Driving Academy represents a leading driving school in Gaborone, Botswana, committed to providing high-quality driving education while ensuring accessibility for students across Gaborone and surrounding areas. Our mission focuses on delivering exceptional service and personalized attention to each student, establishing a comprehensive educational platform that supports both learning objectives and safety standards.

Our Vision

To be Botswana's premier driving education institution, setting the standard for professional instruction, safety training, and student success. We envision a future where every graduate becomes a confident, responsible, and skilled driver contributing to safer roads throughout our nation.

Our Values

  • Safety First: Every aspect of our training prioritizes road safety and defensive driving techniques
  • Professional Excellence: Certified instructors committed to the highest standards of teaching
  • Student-Centered: Personalized attention and flexible scheduling to meet individual needs
  • Community Impact: Contributing to safer roads and responsible driving culture in Botswana
