Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book my first lesson?

You can book through our online form above, call us directly, or visit our academy. We'll discuss your needs and schedule a convenient time.

What should I bring to my first lesson?

Bring a valid ID, learner's permit (if you have one), and comfortable closed shoes. We provide everything else needed for the lesson.

Can I choose my instructor?

Yes! You can request a specific instructor when booking, and we'll do our best to accommodate your preference based on availability.

Do you offer weekend lessons?

Yes, we offer lessons on Saturdays. Some instructors also provide Sunday lessons by special arrangement.
